Bio

I received my B.S. degree Engineering Physics in 2011, and my M.S. degree in Optimization and Systems Theory in 2013, both from KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Stockholm, Sweden. I also have a B.S. in Business and Economics from Stockholm School of Economics in Stockholm, Sweden.

My research is focused on developing formal control methods for safety-critical cyber-physical systems. In particular, I work on algorithms for synthesizing correct-by-construction controllers with applications in the automotive domain.
